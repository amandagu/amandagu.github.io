Mission Statement

Nerds in Training exists to:
1. Spread understanding of what studying STEM is really like by sharing our experiences
Humanize and be unabashedly excited about the things we're learning
Open science to a younger and more diverse audience
Be the nerdy & knowledgeable friend we wished we’d had when starting our undergrads, willing to answer all the “stupid” questions

for anyone:
1. Thinking about pursuing a STEM post-secondary degree or research
2. Trying to figure out what your kid, friend, significant other, or other person in your life actually does
3. Who just wants someone to geek out with
